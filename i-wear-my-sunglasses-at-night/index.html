I wear my sunglasses at night. 🎶

For chill-out reasons on a summer holiday or to disguise on hung over days ... good sunglasses are a must from time to time, also as a style item and fashion statement and for eye protection of course.

Apart from classics like Ray-BanWayfarer, Aviator or  Clubmaster there's also younger models like the new Erika I like very much for it's vintage style, creating a slight retro look. I comes in black, havana (my favorite and the one I got) and brown that actually looks like nude skin.

But it doesn't always have to be a Ray Ban I guess, the Darmstadt (Germany) based shop Klar offers a hand selected variety of original brands of glasses (not sunglasses only) that is always worth a second look. The selected models have strong character and style and you should let the shop owner find your model for you. He'll know.

Also there is still ic! Berlin of course and in terms of style never forget  Mykita. Take a look around.

But why I initially started out to write this post is actually because I discovered Shwood. They started out making sunglasses out of hardwood (wow) and recently I read about their Badlands Collection made out of the strangest (and innovative) of resin cast materials like feathers and desert flowers and on top of that they just look awesome!

Video on the creation of the Badlands Collection to be found here at Solidsmack. Hope you like them too.
